1- how to open an account Once the user has been registered as a member of the icore team, the next step is to open the account, go to

http://www.cs.huji.ac.il/register

and this is what you should get:

fill in with your teudat zehut 8 digits. the next screen will ask for your agreement:

once you agree, an SMS will be sent to your pre-registered cellular phone you will need this number to proceed to the next and final stage:

this will bring you to:

this last step might take a few seconds, and if successful you will see:

once you succeeded in opening the account, to login you will need an OTP (One Time Password). for that you can go to:

http://hotp.cs.huji.ac.il

choose ‘Send sms with otps’, to login here you use the password from step 1

Follow the instructions, and you will get an SMS with 5 OTPs, each consisting of a 5 digit One-Time-Password. You must use them in order, and once the list is exhausted, you must request another batch.

You can also select ‘Register device’, and you will be able to use your smart phone to generate the OTP

for Developers only:

to be able to login, from an *UNIX host

ssh -l user%hm-01 sg.cs.huji.ac.il

host can be any of hm-01, hm-02, hm-04

from a Windows host you can use putty:

"c:\Program Files\putty\putty.exe" -ssh -l user%hm-01 sg.cs.huji.ac.il

in both cases replace ‘user’ by your new login name.

if you are more adventurous you can add -X flag to ssh, and this will open an X11 session to your host.
